What is this

This is a platform game made under 13kb total size - for js13kgames.com programming contest.

Plot

Your king is terribly unlucky... if you help him you may marry your love the beautiful princess Bluehilda.

How to play

PC:

Move right and left with the arrows, jump with space-bar.

Tablet or Phone:

Touch the screen on the right side to jump. To move left touch the screen to the left of the marker (at the bottom left), to move right touch the screen to the right of the marker.
